Selected Research

I'm boardly interested in causal inference, sensitivity analysis, machine learning, and applications in AB testing, finance and biology.

Bounds on the Distribution of a Sum of Two Random Variables: Revisiting a problem of Kolmogorov with application to Individual Treatment Effects.


Zhehao Zhang, Thomas Richardson

We revisit a problem proposed by Kolmogorov. Kolmogorov's problem is closely related to inferring possible distributions for individual treatment effects given the marginal distributions; the latter being identified from a randomized experiment.

Individual Treatment Effect: Predictive Intervals and Sharp Bounds.


Zhehao Zhang, Thomas Richardson

We study the individual treatment effect (ITE) in causal inference settings and provide partial identification results, bounds and prediction intervals and decision-making discussions.

MorPhiC Consortium: towards functional characterization of all human genes.


MorPhiC Consortium (M. Adli, L. Przybyla, W. Sun, Zhehao Zhang et al.),

Worked under Dr. Wei Sun from Fred Hutch Cancer Center to serve as Data Analysis and Validation Centers for The MorPhiC Consortium.

A Comparison of Maxillofacial and Head Injuries Following Electric Scooter and Bicycle Accidents.


Brian Bhaskar, Joshua Alumbaugh, Zhehao Zhang, Jasjit Dillon, Andrea Burke
